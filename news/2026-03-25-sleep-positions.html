Sleep Position Guide After Spine Surgery

March 25, 2026 · Recovery

Getting quality sleep after spine surgery can be challenging. Our rehabilitation specialists share their top recommendations for comfortable sleeping positions during recovery.

Best Sleep Positions

  • On your back with pillow under knees: Reduces pressure on lumbar spine
  • Side sleeping with pillow between knees: Keeps spine aligned
  • Recliner position: Helpful for first 2 weeks if back sleeping is uncomfortable

What to Avoid

  • Stomach sleeping (puts 200% more pressure on lumbar discs)
  • Thin mattresses that allow spine to sag
  • Old pillows that don't support neck curve

Recommended Products

Our care coordinators can arrange delivery of medical-grade pillows and mattresses to your recovery accommodation.

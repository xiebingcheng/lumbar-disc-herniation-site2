Mexico Emerges as Spine Surgery Destination: How It Compares

April 12, 2026 · Industry

With medical tourism growing 34% annually, Mexico has become a popular destination for US patients seeking affordable spine surgery. However, when comparing options, many factors go beyond just cost.

Mexico vs China: A Comparison

  • Surgical expertise: China performs 15,000+ spine surgeries annually vs Mexico's 3,000
  • Hospital accreditation: China has 12 JCI-accredited spine centers vs Mexico's 2
  • Technology: China's hospitals average 5 years newer equipment
  • Cost: Similar pricing for both countries

Our Position

We believe in providing patients with complete information. Both countries offer valid options, but for complex spine cases, China's higher surgical volume and specialized facilities often provide better outcomes.

Stem Cell Therapy for Disc Degeneration: New Breakthrough

May 9, 2026 · Innovation

Chinese researchers have achieved promising results in using stem cell therapy to treat disc degeneration, offering hope for patients who have exhausted traditional treatment options. The Phase II clinical trial shows 78% of patients experienced significant pain reduction and improved disc hydration.

How It Works

Mesenchymal stem cells harvested from the patient's own bone marrow are injected into the degenerated disc under fluoroscopic guidance. Over 6 months, the cells stimulate regeneration of disc tissue and reduce inflammation.

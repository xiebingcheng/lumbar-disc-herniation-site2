Acupressure for Post-Spine Surgery Recovery: Does It Help?

April 15, 2026 · Recovery

A growing number of spine surgery patients are exploring complementary therapies like acupressure to manage postoperative pain and accelerate recovery. We interviewed leading rehabilitation specialists about the evidence behind these approaches.

The Research

Studies from Beijing University of Chinese Medicine show that acupressure applied to specific points can reduce reliance on opioid pain medications by up to 35%. The technique works by stimulating the body's natural pain-relief mechanisms.

Our Recommendations

We recommend acupressure as a complement to, not replacement for, conventional pain management. Our partner hospitals offer integrated recovery programs that combine Western rehabilitation with traditional Chinese medicine techniques.

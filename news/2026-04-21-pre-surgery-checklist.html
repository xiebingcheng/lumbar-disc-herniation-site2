Pre-Surgery Checklist: Everything You Need to Prepare

April 21, 2026 · Service

Proper preparation for spine surgery can significantly impact your outcome and recovery speed. This comprehensive checklist covers medical, travel, and personal preparations to ensure a smooth experience.

Medical Preparation

  • Complete all required MRI and blood tests
  • Stop blood thinners as directed by your surgeon
  • Disclose all medications and supplements
  • Quit smoking at least 2 weeks before surgery

Travel Arrangements

  • Book flights with flexibility for date changes
  • Arrange airport transfers with our team
  • Prepare passport with 6+ months validity
  • Arrange leave from work for 3-4 weeks

What to Pack

  • Comfortable loose clothing
  • Slip-on shoes
  • All medical records and imaging
  • Contact information for your local physician

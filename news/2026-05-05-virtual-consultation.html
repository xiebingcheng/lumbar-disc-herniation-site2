Free Virtual Consultation Service Now Available

myMedVia now offers free initial virtual consultations for patients considering treatment abroad. Our medical coordinators connect you with spine specialists within 24 hours of your request.

How It Works

  1. Submit Request: Fill out our contact form with your medical information and MRI reports
  2. Medical Review: Our team reviews your case and connects you with an appropriate specialist
  3. Virtual Meeting: Have a video consultation with the surgeon to discuss your condition and options
  4. Treatment Plan: Receive a detailed treatment plan and cost estimate
  5. Travel Planning: If you choose to proceed, we handle all logistics

What's Included in the Free Consultation

  • Medical record review by our coordination team
  • Surgeon matching based on your specific condition
  • 30-minute video consultation with a spine specialist
  • Preliminary treatment recommendations
  • Cost estimate for surgery and recovery
  • Answers to your questions about the process

Who Should Request a Virtual Consultation

This service is ideal for patients who:

  • Have been diagnosed with lumbar disc herniation or other spine conditions
  • Are facing long wait times for surgery in their home country
  • Want to explore affordable treatment options abroad
  • Need a second opinion on their diagnosis or treatment plan
  • Want to understand their options before making a decision

Technology and Privacy

Our virtual consultations use secure, HIPAA-compliant video platforms. All medical information is encrypted and handled with strict confidentiality. Your privacy is our priority.

Languages Supported

Consultations are available in English, Spanish, French, German, Russian, Arabic, and Mandarin. interpreters can be arranged for additional languages.

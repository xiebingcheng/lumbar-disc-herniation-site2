Walking Program: 12-Week Progressive Schedule After Surgery

March 16, 2026 · Recovery

Walking is one of the best exercises for spine surgery recovery. Our physical therapy team has developed a progressive 12-week walking program designed to safely build endurance and strength.

Week-by-Week Progression

  • Weeks 1-2: 5-10 minutes, 3-4 times daily, flat surfaces only
  • Weeks 3-4: 15-20 minutes, 2-3 times daily
  • Weeks 5-6: 25-30 minutes, twice daily
  • Weeks 7-8: 35-40 minutes, once daily with hills
  • Weeks 9-12: 45+ minutes, incorporating stairs and varied terrain

Warning Signs

Reduce activity if you experience increased pain, numbness, or weakness. Contact our care team with any concerns during your recovery.

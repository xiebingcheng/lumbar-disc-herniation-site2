Yoga Positions Safe After Lumbar Disc Surgery

April 6, 2026 · Recovery

Many patients ask about returning to yoga practice after spine surgery. Physical therapists at our partner hospitals have compiled a guide to safe poses that promote flexibility without risking complications.

Safe Poses (After 12 Weeks)

  • Cat-cow stretches for spine mobility
  • Child's pose for gentle stretching
  • Supine knee-to-chest for nerve gliding
  • Bridge pose (modified) for glute strengthening

Positions to Avoid

  • Deep forward folds (until 6 months)
  • Twisting poses involving spine rotation
  • Backbends that compress lumbar discs
  • Inverted poses until full fusion confirmation

Always consult your surgeon before starting any exercise program.

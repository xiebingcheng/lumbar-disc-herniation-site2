Real-Time Post-Op Monitoring via Smartband Technology

April 3, 2026 · Innovation

Our partner hospitals have integrated smartband technology into post-operative care, allowing surgeons to monitor patient recovery metrics in real-time even after discharge from the hospital.

How It Works

Patients wear a medical-grade smartband that tracks incision site temperature, movement patterns, heart rate variability, and sleep quality. Data is transmitted securely to the care team, with automatic alerts for any concerning trends.

Benefits

  • Earlier detection of potential complications
  • Reduced need for in-person follow-up visits
  • More personalized recovery guidance
  • Peace of mind for patients traveling home

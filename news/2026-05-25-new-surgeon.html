Dr. Wei Zhang Joins Our Medical Advisory Board

We are proud to announce that Dr. Wei Zhang, a renowned spine surgeon with over 15 years of experience in endoscopic discectomy, has joined our medical advisory board to help guide treatment protocols for international patients.

About Dr. Wei Zhang

Dr. Zhang completed his medical education at Peking Union Medical College before specializing in spine surgery at the University of Tokyo. He has performed over 4,000 endoscopic discectomy procedures and pioneered several minimally invasive techniques now used worldwide.

Professional Achievements

  • 15+ years of dedicated spine surgery practice
  • 4,000+ successful endoscopic discectomies
  • 3 patents for minimally invasive surgical instruments
  • 50+ publications in peer-reviewed spine surgery journals
  • Founder of the minimally invasive spine surgery training program at Peking Union Medical College Hospital

Expertise Areas

Dr. Zhang specializes in:

  • Endoscopic lumbar discectomy (YESS and TESSYS techniques)
  • Percutaneous transforaminal endoscopic disc surgery
  • Treatment of recurrent disc herniation
  • Complex spinal stenosis decompression
  • Navigation-guided minimally invasive spine surgery

Statement from Dr. Zhang

"I have dedicated my career to making minimally invasive spine surgery accessible to more patients. Through my work with myMedVia, I can help international patients benefit from techniques that reduce recovery time and improve outcomes. Every patient deserves access to world-class spine care regardless of where they live."

Impact on International Patient Care

Dr. Zhang's involvement with our medical advisory board will enhance:

  • Treatment Planning: His expertise ensures complex cases receive comprehensive evaluation
  • Surgeon Network: He'll help train and credential additional spine surgeons in our partner network
  • Protocol Development: Establishing standardized care pathways for international patients
  • Second Opinions: Offering virtual consultations for patients seeking alternative treatment perspectives

Mental Health Impact of Chronic Back Pain: Support Resources

March 19, 2026 · Healthcare

Chronic back pain doesn't just affect the body—it takes a significant toll on mental health. Studies show that chronic pain patients are three times more likely to experience depression and anxiety. Our comprehensive approach addresses both physical and psychological aspects of recovery.

Common Mental Health Challenges

  • Depression and anxiety related to pain and limited activity
  • Frustration with healthcare system wait times
  • Strain on personal and professional relationships
  • Fear about surgical outcomes and recovery

Medical Tourism and Travel: Visa Requirements for China

March 10, 2026 · Service

Understanding China's visa requirements is essential for planning your medical trip. Our team assists with the entire visa process, including the medical visa (F visa) which allows longer stays for treatment.

Visa Options

  • Tourist visa (L): Valid for 30-60 days, suitable for short treatments
  • Medical visa (F): Valid for 90 days, extendable, ideal for surgery and recovery
  • Transit visa (G): For brief layovers only

Application Requirements

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Invitation letter from hospital (we provide this)
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Travel insurance documentation
  • Bank statement showing sufficient funds

Our visa coordination team has helped thousands of patients navigate this process successfully.

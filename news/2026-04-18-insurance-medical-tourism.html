Insurance Company Launches Medical Tourism Coverage Option

April 18, 2026 · Industry

Major international insurance provider GlobalCare has announced a new medical tourism rider that covers spine surgery in China for policyholders. This marks a significant shift in insurance industry attitudes toward overseas medical treatment.

Coverage Details

The new rider covers up to $15,000 for spine surgery at JCI-accredited hospitals in China, including travel and accommodation costs. Policyholders pay a 15% higher premium but save on deductible costs compared to domestic surgery.

Why Insurance Companies Are Changing

With employer healthcare costs rising 12% annually, insurers see medical tourism as a way to reduce payouts while providing patients with faster access to quality care. GlobalCare estimates savings of $8,000 per covered spine surgery.

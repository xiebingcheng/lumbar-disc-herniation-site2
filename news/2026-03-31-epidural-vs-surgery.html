Epidural Steroid Injections vs Surgery: When to Choose

March 31, 2026 · Treatment

Patients with disc herniation often face a choice between epidural steroid injections and surgical intervention. Understanding when each treatment is appropriate can help you make a more informed decision.

When Injections May Be Preferred

  • First episode of radicular pain
  • Mild to moderate disc herniation
  • Patient prefers to delay surgery
  • Surgical risk factors present

When Surgery Is Recommended

  • Severe nerve compression with weakness
  • Cauda equina syndrome symptoms
  • Failed 6+ weeks of conservative treatment
  • Significant disc extrusion or sequestration

Our medical team will provide a clear recommendation based on your MRI findings and symptoms.

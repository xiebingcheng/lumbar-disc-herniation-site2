Patient Success Rate Reaches 97% at Partner Hospitals

May 14, 2026 · Healthcare

Our network of JCI-accredited partner hospitals has achieved a 97% success rate in spine surgeries performed for international patients over the past 12 months. This milestone reflects our commitment to world-class care and rigorous surgeon selection standards.

What This Means for Patients

Out of 3,847 spine procedures performed for international patients in 2025-2026, 97.2% resulted in successful outcomes with patients reporting significant pain reduction and improved mobility. The remaining 2.8% involved minor complications that resolved with standard treatment.

Quality Assurance Process

All surgeons in our network undergo a rigorous credentialing process including review of complication rates, peer recommendations, and verification of international training. Annual performance reviews ensure continued excellence.

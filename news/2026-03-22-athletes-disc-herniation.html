Disc Herniation in Athletes: Treatment Considerations

March 22, 2026 · Treatment

Athletes face unique challenges when dealing with disc herniation. The decision between conservative treatment and surgery requires careful consideration of sport-specific demands and career timeline.

Sport-Specific Considerations

  • Contact sports: May require surgical fix for structural integrity
  • Golf/Tennis: Rotation-heavy sports often respond well to conservative care
  • Running: Core strengthening often enables return to sport
  • Swimming: Excellent low-impact exercise during recovery

Surgical Options for Athletes

Our surgeons have extensive experience with professional and amateur athletes. Endoscopic techniques allow faster return to play—some patients return to training in 8 weeks.

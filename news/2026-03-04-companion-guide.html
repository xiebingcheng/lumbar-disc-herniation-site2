Companion Travel Guide: Supporting a Spine Surgery Patient

March 4, 2026 · Service

If you're planning to accompany a loved one for spine surgery in China, this guide will help you prepare for the journey and be an effective support person during their recovery.

Before the Trip

  • Arrange flexible return flights (recovery can be faster or slower than expected)
  • Pack comfortable, loose clothing for the patient
  • Bring entertainment options (tablets, books) for recovery downtime
  • Prepare questions for the medical team

During the Hospital Stay

  • Learn the warning signs of complications
  • Assist with mobility as allowed by medical team
  • Take notes during doctor consultations
  • Provide emotional support and encouragement

Recovery Accommodation

We arrange companion-friendly accommodations with kitchen facilities, ensuring comfort for both patient and companion during the recovery period.

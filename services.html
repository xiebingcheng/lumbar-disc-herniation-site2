Your Patient Journey

A clear step-by-step process from initial contact to full recovery.

1. Free Consultation

Submit your MRI images and medical history through our secure portal. Our medical team reviews your case within 24 hours and provides a preliminary treatment recommendation with cost estimates.

2. Treatment Plan Confirmation

Review your personalized treatment plan, meet your surgeon via video call, and confirm your procedure dates. We provide visa invitation letters and travel itinerary within 48 hours of confirmation.

3. Travel to China

Our team meets you at the airport. Transportation to your hotel or hospital accommodation is arranged. A medical coordinator is available 24/7 throughout your stay.

4. Pre-Operative Assessment

Complete physical examination, final MRI if needed, and pre-surgery consultations. Your surgeon explains the procedure and answers all questions before you sign consent forms.

5. Surgery & Hospital Stay

State-of-the-art surgical facilities with private rooms. Minimally invasive techniques mean smaller incisions, less pain, and faster recovery. Average hospital stay: 3-5 days.

6. Recovery & Return

Guided physical therapy begins in the hospital. Follow-up appointments monitor your healing. We arrange medical travel documentation and coordinate with your local doctor for continued care back home.

Most patients complete their treatment within 2-3 weeks total. This includes initial consultations (2-3 days), pre-operative assessments (1-2 days), surgery (1 day), and hospital stay (3-5 days). If you need extended recovery time, we can arrange extended accommodation.

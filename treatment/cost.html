Surgery Cost Breakdown

Prices are all-inclusive and cover the entire treatment journey from admission to discharge. All surgeries are performed by board-certified spine surgeons at JCI-accredited hospitals.

🔬

Microdiscectomy

$3,000–$5,000

Minimally invasive removal of herniated disc material pressing on nerve roots. Most common procedure with 95%+ success rate.

  • ✓ Duration: 45–90 minutes
  • ✓ Hospital stay: 1–2 nights
  • ✓ Recovery: 2–4 weeks
🎯

Endoscopic Discectomy

$4,000–$6,500

Advanced minimally invasive technique using a small endoscope. Smaller incision, faster recovery, less post-operative pain.

  • ✓ Duration: 60–90 minutes
  • ✓ Hospital stay: 1 night
  • ✓ Recovery: 1–2 weeks
⚙️

Spinal Fusion

$8,000–$12,000

For severe cases or disc degeneration. Joins two or more vertebrae for stability. Used when other treatments are not sufficient.

  • ✓ Duration: 2–4 hours
  • ✓ Hospital stay: 3–5 nights
  • ✓ Recovery: 6–12 weeks

Insurance & Cost FAQ

Most international insurance plans do not cover treatment abroad. However, our patients often find that even paying out-of-pocket for treatment in China is significantly cheaper than insurance co-pays, deductibles, and unmet limits in their home country. We can provide detailed invoices and medical documentation for you to submit to your insurance provider for potential reimbursement. Contact us to discuss your specific insurance situation.

We believe in complete transparency. Your quoted price includes everything listed in your package: surgeon fees, hospital stay, medications, transfers, and accommodation. There are no hidden charges. Any additional pre-existing conditions or complications discovered during surgery that require extended care will be discussed with you before proceeding. We provide a detailed cost breakdown before you commit.

Every surgical case is unique. While complications are rare (less than 2% for microdiscectomy), if follow-up treatment or revision surgery is needed within 6 months of your original procedure, we will coordinate the additional care at no extra surgeon fees. Hospital facility fees for any revision within the initial recovery period would be quoted separately.

We accept bank transfers, credit cards (with small processing fee), and PayPal. A 30% deposit is required to confirm your treatment dates, with the balance due upon arrival at the hospital before surgery. All payments are documented with full invoices for your records.

Yes. Our partner hospitals are JCI-accredited (Joint Commission International), meaning they meet the same global healthcare standards as top Western hospitals. Your spine surgeon will be board-certified with 15+ years of experience and has trained internationally. We only partner with top-tier academic hospitals in major Chinese cities with state-of-the-art equipment.

What Happens Next?

After you submit your inquiry, our patient coordination team will review your information and contact you within 24 hours.

Our Process:

  1. Initial Review: Our medical team reviews your case and MRI/CT scans if available.
  2. Treatment Plan: We provide personalized treatment recommendations from our partner spine surgeons.
  3. Cost Estimate: Receive a detailed breakdown of treatment costs, accommodation, and travel arrangements.
  4. Travel Coordination: We assist with medical visa invitation letters, airport transfers, and accommodation booking.
  5. Treatment & Recovery: Receive ongoing support throughout your treatment and follow-up care.

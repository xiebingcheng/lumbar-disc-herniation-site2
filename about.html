Our Story

myMedVia was born from a simple but powerful observation: millions of people worldwide suffer from lumbar disc herniation, and too many wait months — sometimes years — for the surgery that could change their lives.

Our founder, Dr. Wei Zhang, is a former orthopedic surgeon who witnessed firsthand how international patients struggled to access timely spinal care. After years of practicing medicine in Beijing, he realized that China had developed world-class spine surgery capabilities — yet most international patients never knew these options existed.

So in 2019, myMedVia was created to solve this problem. We built a service that bridges the gap between international patients and China's top spine surgeons, handling everything from medical records translation to post-treatment follow-up. Our goal is simple: get you the treatment you need, when you need it, at a price that doesn't break the bank.

Today, we've helped patients from 28 countries receive life-changing spine surgery in China. Every patient we help is a reminder of why we exist.

Our Mission & Values

Compassion

We understand that back pain affects every aspect of your life — your work, your family, your mood. We treat every patient as we would want our own family treated: with empathy, patience, and genuine care.

Transparency

No hidden fees. No vague promises. We provide clear cost estimates, honest success rates, and detailed treatment timelines. You deserve to know exactly what to expect before you travel.

Excellence

We only partner with hospitals that meet international accreditation standards and employ surgeons with proven track records. Your safety and outcomes are non-negotiable.

Speed

While your home country queues for months, we can often schedule your MRI, consultation, and surgery within days of your arrival in China. Time is precious when you're in pain.
